A brief introduction to DEM

Dennilton Educational Movement(DEM) is a non-profit organisation that is based in the area of Dennilton at Sekhukhune district in the Limpopo province.

Note: DEM is mainly operational in the area of Dennilton, but is willing to help any learner that may not fall within the region. This was demonstrated when we conducted online classes amid the covid-19 pandemic. We were able to help learners as far as Eastern Cape.
